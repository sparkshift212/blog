Mama & Baby Bear Pattern

By Sarah Weaver • June 2026 • Free Pattern
Share:

This little guy works up super fast and uses only basic stitches, making it the perfect weekend project for beginners!

Materials Needed

  • Worsted weight yarn in Light Pink and Hot Pink
  • 4.0mm (G) crochet hook
  • 9mm safety eyes
  • Polyfil stuffing
  • Yarn needle and scissors

Pattern Instructions

Head & Body (Make 1 in Light Pink)

R1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
R2: inc in each st around (12)
R3: *sc, inc* around (18)
R4: *2 sc, inc* around (24)
R5-8: sc in each st around (24)
Place safety eyes between R6 and R7, 5 stitches apart.

Gills (Make 6 in Hot Pink)

Ch 4, sl st in 2nd ch from hook, sc in next, hdc in last. Fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.

Assembly

Sew three gills to each side of the head, right behind the eyes. Embroider a tiny little smile with black embroidery floss. Stuff the body firmly before closing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sell the finished plushie?

Yes! You are welcome to sell the finished items made from this pattern, provided you credit Yarnoodle Amigurumi as the designer.

What yarn is best for this?

I highly recommend using chenille or velvet yarn for a cuddly finish, but worsted weight cotton works great for a smaller, more structured plushie!
